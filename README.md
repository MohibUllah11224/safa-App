# Safa App Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **Safa App** ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is developed and maintained by **Mohib Ullah** ("we", "us", "our").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password
- Username


This information is used for authentication and to uniquely identify users within the App.

**Privacy of Reports:** Each user can only view their own reports. Users cannot see other users’ reports, and only authorized administrators have access to all submitted complaints for management and processing.

### b. Location Information (GPS)
The App collects your device’s GPS location to allow users to mark the exact position of trash or waste within the city. This information is used only for reporting purposes to help authorities clean the area.

### c. Camera Access
The App uses your device’s camera to take photos of trash that needs to be cleaned. Photos are used only for reporting and improving the accuracy of the report.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To authenticate your account using Firebase Authentication
- To submit trash reports to the city authority
- To ensure **user privacy**, allowing only admins to view and edit all reports
- To improve accuracy of service requests
- To help city authorities locate and clean waste more efficiently

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, selling to third parties, or tracking outside the app.

---

## 3. Data Storage
All user account data (email, username, phone number), photos, and location reports are securely stored in **Google Firebase**, a cloud service. Data is encrypted and protected according to Google’s security standards.

---

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:
- The relevant city authority responsible for waste management
- Google Firebase (only as a storage provider)

We never sell or rent user data.

---

## 5. Permissions Used
- **Location (GPS):** To record the location of trash reports
- **Camera:** To take photos for reporting purposes
- **Contacts & Phone (optional):** Only if needed for account verification

Permissions are used only when needed for the intended features.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

---

## 7. Security
We take data security seriously and use industry-standard measures to protect user data. Firebase Authentication ensures your account credentials are safely stored and encrypted.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted online once a webpage version is published.
